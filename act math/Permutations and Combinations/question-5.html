A committee will be selected from a group of 12 women and 18 men. The committee will consist of 5 women and 5 men. Which of the following expressions gives the number of different committees that could be selected from these 30 people?

Approach

Since we are choosing groups, order does not really matter (it doesn't matter who is first in a group).

To select the women, we choose 5 members from 12:

$$ {}_{12}C_{5} $$

To select the men, we choose 5 members from 18:

$$ {}_{18}C_{5} $$

Each possible combination of women will be paired with each possible combination of men to get the total possible committees.

$$ ({}_{12}C_{5})({}_{18}C_{5}) $$
