A spin of a certain spinner has 5 possible outcomes. The table below shows the probability of spinning each of the 5 outcomes. The probability of spinning Outcome 4 is \(a\) and the probability of spinning Outcome 5 is \(2a\). What is the value of \(a\) ?

$$ \begin{array}{|l|c|c|c|c|c|} \hline \text{Outcome} & 1 & 2 & 3 & 4 & 5 \\ \hline \text{Probability} & 0.20 & 0.38 & 0.24 & a & 2a \\ \hline \end{array} $$
Approach

The sum of all the probabilities of all of the outcomes needs to equal 1.

$$ 0.20 + 0.38 + 0.24 + a + 2a = 1 $$ $$ 0.82 + 3a = 1 $$ $$ 3a = 0.18 $$ $$ a = \boxed{0.06} $$
