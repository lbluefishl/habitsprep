The temperature in Chicago was \(-4^\circ\)F at noon and rose \(24^\circ\)F by 3:00 p.m. The temperature in New Orleans was \(42^\circ\)F at noon and dropped \(24^\circ\)F by 3:00 p.m. How did the temperatures in Chicago and New Orleans compare at 3:00 p.m. ?

Approach
Chicago
$$-4 + 24 = 20$$
New Orleans
$$ 42-24 = 18 $$
