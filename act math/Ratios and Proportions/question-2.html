Katerina runs \(15\) miles in \(2\dfrac{1}{2}\) hours. What is the average number of minutes it takes her to run \(1\) mile?

Approach

We can set up a proportion, as long as we keep the units consistent. Changing \(2\frac{1}{2}\) hours into \(150\) minutes is appropriate.

$$ \frac{x \text{ minutes}}{1 \text{ mile}} = \frac{150 \text{ minutes}}{15 \text{ miles}} $$ $$ x=\boxed{10} $$
