Which of the following expressions is equal to \((\sin{60^\circ})(\cos{30^\circ})+(\cos{60^\circ})(\sin{30^\circ})\) ?

Approach 1

If you know the identity, this problem is straightforward:

$$ \sin{(\alpha + \beta)} = \sin{\alpha}\cos{\beta} + \cos{\alpha}\sin{\beta} $$
