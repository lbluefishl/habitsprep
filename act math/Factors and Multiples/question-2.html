Coach Shannon is buying packages of granola bars, juice boxes, and apples as snacks for her soccer team. The table below gives the number of snacks per package and the price per package.

What is the minimum total price of the snacks, all bought in whole packages, Coach Shannon buys so that each of the 15 girls on the team gets at least 1 snack of each type?

Approach

We need at least 15 of each.

$$ \text{Granola bars} : 3, 6, 9, 12, 15 $$ $$ \text{Juice Boxes} : 4, 8, 12, 16 $$ $$ \text{Apples} : 5, 10, 15 $$ $$ 5(\$2.50) + 4(\$3.00) + 3(\$4.50) $$ $$ = 12.5 + 12 + 13.5 $$ $$ = \boxed{38} $$
