All 25 students in a chemistry class took a test. Each student earned a test score that was an integer number of points, and no 2 students earned the same test score. The median test score was 80 points. How many students earned a test score that was greater than 80 points?

Approach

The median of 25 values is the 13th value. Therefore 12 students had to earn a score greater then the 13th student.
