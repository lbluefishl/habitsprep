Sani's course grade in his chemistry class is based on 3 tests and 1 final exam. Each of the 3 test scores is weighted as 20% of the course grade, and the final exam score is weighted as 40% of the course grade. Sani's 3 test scores are 78, 86, and 82, respectively. What is the minimum score that Sani will have to earn on the final exam in order to receive a course grade of at least 86 ?
Approach

We calculate the weighted value of each test by summing each score with its weight (percentage):

$$ 78(0.2) + 86(0.2) + 82(0.2) + x(0.4) \geq 86 $$ $$ 49.2+0.4x \geq 86 $$ $$ 0.4x \geq 36.8 $$ $$ x \geq \boxed{92} $$
