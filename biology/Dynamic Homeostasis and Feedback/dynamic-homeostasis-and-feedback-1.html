The endocrine system incorporates feedback mechanisms that maintain homeostasis. Which of the following demonstrates negative feedback by the endocrine system?

Approach

Negative feedback occurs when a bodily process works to counteract the direction of another process. The answer choice correctly shows a process in which glucose levels are reduced when they are high.

The option involving contractions is an example of positive feedback since initial pressure causes further contractions.

The other two options are not feedback mechanisms which maintain homeostasis and do not involve the endocrine system.
