Approach

Since AAS behaves the same way as testosterone, we expect similar interactions. Testosterone inhibits the hypothalamus and anterior pituitary. This results in less LH and FSH, which results in less stimulation of the Leydig cells and Sertoli cells. This further results in less stimulation of the germ cells. Ultimately, this leads to reduced sperm production.
