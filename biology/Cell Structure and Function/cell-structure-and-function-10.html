Which of the following best describes an advantage that eukaryote organisms have over prokaryote organisms?

Approach

Eukaryotes show greater complexity in that they have organelles specialized for specific functions. For example, ATP production is carried out in the mitochondria.

The other choices are incorrect or do not describe an advantage. Prokaryotes do have cell membranes. Mutations are typically not advantageous for the cell. Having individual genomes for organelles will increase the time it takes to copy the cell.
