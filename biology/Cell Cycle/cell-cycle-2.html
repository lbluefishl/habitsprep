Cancer can result from a variety of different mutational events. Which of the following is LEAST likely to result in the initiation of a cancerous tumor?

Approach

If there is a defect which causes the cell to stall at the stage prior to the S phase, then the cell cycle itself will stall. S phase is one of the requirements prior to cell division. Not being able to pass this checkpoint means that the cell will never divide. Uncontrolled cell growth and division are unlikely to occur if the cell cannot divide.
