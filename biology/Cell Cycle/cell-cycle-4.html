A new mutation that arose in one copy of gene X in a somatic cell resulted in the formation of a tumor. Which of the following pieces of evidence best describes how the new mutation directly caused the tumor?

Approach

Tumors are the result of uncontrolled cell growth and division. If Protein X stimulates cell division, an overactive protein would definately overstimulate cell division.

All of the other choices would reduce cell division, corresponding to lower rates of cancer.
