High blood cholesterol (hypercholesterolemia, HC) can lead to cardiovascular problems such as atherosclerosis and heart attack. Exercise and monitoring of diet can often control cholesterol levels; however, in certain cases HC is inherited as an autosomal dominant disease caused by a mutation in a single gene.

A man with high cholesterol levels is about to marry a woman whose total cholesterol levels are also higher than average. A physician has suggested they get tested for the \(HC\) allele. Which of the following is a valid ethical question concerning the test?

Approach

Many medical test results may lead to ethical issues, which involve questions of right or wrong. A controversial issue is that of health care premiums. If insurance companies have access to medical records, is it ethical to make more money from these people knowing they are at greater risk of getting sick?

Health, diet, and exercise aren't ethical issues by themselves.
