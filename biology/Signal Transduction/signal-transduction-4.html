Approach

The hormone binds with a protein and forms a hormone-receptor complex. This complex moves into the nucleus and promotes transcription, resulting in the production of mRNA.

There is no evidence that histone proteins or ribosomes are being made.

Similarly, there is no evidence that DNA is replicated in response to the complex.
