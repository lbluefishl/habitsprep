For a sample of 42 rabbits, the mean weight is 5 pounds and the standard deviation of weights is 3 pounds. Which of the following is most likely true about the weights for the rabbits in this sample?

Approach

Most rabbit will likely be between 2-8 pounds, or within one standard deviation of the mean. However, it is unlikely we ill get too many rabbits below 2 pounds. It is impossible to get lower weight rabbits as well. It is possible to have heavier rabbits 2 or more stnadard deviations to the right of the mean. Hence, there is the likelihood of a tail in the distribution to the right of the mean.
