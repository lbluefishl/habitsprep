If \( 3y-x=10 \), what is the value of \(\dfrac{27^y}{3^x} \) ?

Approach

We should notice that \(\dfrac{27^y}{3^y} \) might be simplifiable. To use any of the exponent rules, we must rewrite with equivalent bases.

$$ \frac{27^y}{3^x} $$ $$ =\frac{(3^3)^y}{3^x} $$ $$ =\frac{3^{3y}}{3^x} $$ $$ = 3^{3y-x} $$ $$ =\boxed{3^{10}} $$
