$$ y \leq 5x-3 $$ $$ 2x-4 \geq 12+y $$

In the \(xy\)-plane, point \(A\) is contained in the graph of the solution set of the system of inequalities above. Which of the following could be the coordinates of point \(A\) ?

Approach

The primary way to approach this question is to test the points. For the point to be contained in the solution set, it must satisfy both inequalities.

\(\colorbox{aqua}{$(-4,2)$}\)
$$ 2 \leq 5(-4)-3 $$ $$ 2 \leq -23   ✖ $$ $$ 2(-4)-4 \geq 12+2 $$ $$ -12 \geq 14   ✖$$
\(\colorbox{aqua}{$(1,1)$}\)
$$ 1 \leq 5(1)-3 $$ $$ 1 \leq 2   \checkmark$$ $$ 2(1)-4 \geq 12+1 $$ $$ -2 \geq 13   ✖$$
\(\colorbox{aqua}{$(3,-10)$}\)
$$ -10 \leq 5(3)-3 $$ $$ -10 \leq 12   \checkmark $$ $$ 2(3)-4 \geq 12+(-10) $$ $$ 2 \geq 2   \checkmark $$
\(\colorbox{aqua}{$(6,2)$}\)
$$ 2 \leq 5(6)-3 $$ $$ 2 \leq 27   \checkmark $$ $$ 2(6)-4 \geq 12+2 $$ $$ 8 \geq 14   ✖$$
