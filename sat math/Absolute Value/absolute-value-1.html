If \( | 2x+5 |=7 \) and \(| 3y+6 | = 12\), which of the following is not a possible value of \( |xy| \) ?

Approach

Solving for \(x \text{ and } y\):

\(2x+5=7\)
$$ 2x=2 $$ $$ x=1 $$
\(2x+5=-7\)
$$ 2x=-12 $$ $$ x=-6 $$
\(3y+6=12 \)
$$ 3y=6 $$ $$ y=2 $$
\(3y+6=-12\)
$$ 3y=-18 $$ $$ y=-6 $$
$$ x=1 \text{ or } -6 $$ $$ y=2 \text{ or } -6 $$

Possible values of \(xy\) are:

$$ 1 \cdot 2 =2$$ $$ 1 \cdot -6 = -6 $$ $$ -6 \cdot 2 = -12 $$ $$ -6 \cdot -6 = 36 $$
