The number of people who went to a certain store each day of the week is shown in the table below.

$$ \begin{array}{|l|c|} \hline \hspace{2em} \text{Day} & \text{Number of customers} \\ \hline \text{ Monday} & 113 \\ \hline \text{ Tuesday} & 93 \\ \hline \text{ Wednesday} & 85 \\ \hline \text{ Thursday} & 90 \\ \hline \text{ Friday} & 67 \\ \hline \text{ Saturday} & 132 \\ \hline \text{ Sunday} & 55 \\ \hline \end{array} $$

Which of the following is true based on these data?

Approach

Rather than risking a careless mistake, we should confirm that three choices are incorrect.

The store had most of its customers on Saturday.

From Monday to Friday, the number of customers increased from Wednesday to Thursday.

The number of customers on Wednesday and Thursday combined is \(85+90=175\). The number of customers on Friday and Saturday combined is \(67+132=199\).
