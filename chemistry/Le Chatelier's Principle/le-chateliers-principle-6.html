For which of the following reaction mixtures at equilibrium would reducing the volume of the container at constant temperature cause the equilibrium to shift toward the products?

Approach

As the volume of a container decreases, the pressure of the gases will increase. The equilibrium position will shift to counter this change, by trying to shift to a position so that pressure decreases. To decrease the pressure, we want less molecules of gas in the container. In the last option, the forward reaction results in less molecules of gas (2 to 1).
