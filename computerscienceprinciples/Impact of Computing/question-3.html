An author is considering publishing an e-book using a Creative Commons license. In which of the following situations would it be better for the author to use a Creative Commons license instead of a tradtional copyright?

  1. The author wants to make the e-book available as a free download.
  2. The author wants to prevent people from sharing copies of the e-book on peer-to-peer networks.
  3. The author wants to allow people permission to use and modify the e-book.
Approach

A Creative Commons license is designed so that the information is available for public use and modification. The author would want the book to be shared in peer-to-peer networks.
