For time \(t\geq 0\), the height \(h\) of an object suspended from a spring is given by \(h(t)=5+8\cos\left(\dfrac{\pi t}{4}\right)\). What is the average height of the object from \(t=0\) to \(t=2\) ?

Approach

The average value of a function is:

$$ \frac{1}{b-a}\int\limits_a^b f(x)   dx $$ $$ \frac{1}{2-0}\int\limits_0^2 5+8\cos\left(\frac{\pi t}{4}\right)   dt $$ $$ = \frac{1}{2} \left[ 5t+\frac{32}{\pi}\sin\left(\frac{\pi t}{4}\right) \right]_0^2 $$ $$ = \frac{1}{2} \left[ \left(5(2)+\frac{32}{\pi}\sin\Big(\frac{\pi(2)}{4}\Big)\right)-\left(5(0)-\frac{32}{\pi}\sin\Big(\frac{\pi(0)}{4}\Big)\right) \right] $$ $$ = \frac{1}{2}\left[ \left(10+\frac{32}{\pi}\right) - \left( 0-0\right) \right] $$ $$ = \boxed{5+\frac{16}{\pi}} $$
