The figure above shows the graph of a function \(f\) with domain \(0\leq x\leq 4\). Which of the following statements are true?

  • \(\hskip{1em}\lim\limits_{x\to 2^-}f(x)\) exists.
  • \(\hskip{1em}\lim\limits_{x\to 2^+}f(x)\) exists.
  • \(\hskip{1em}\lim\limits_{x\to 2}f(x)\) exists.
Approach

Recall that the limit exists if the left and right hand limits equal each other. From the graph,

$$ \lim\limits_{x\to 2^-}f(x) \neq \lim\limits_{x\to 2^+}f(x) $$
