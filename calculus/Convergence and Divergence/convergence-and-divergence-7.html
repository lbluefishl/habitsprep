If the series \(\displaystyle \sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n \) converges and \(a_n \gt 0 \) for all \(n\), which of the following must be true?

Approach

The last two choices refer to the direct comparison test.

Since \(\dfrac{a_n}{n}\) is always less than \(a_n\), \(\dfrac{a_n}{n}\) must converge since \(a_n\) converges.
