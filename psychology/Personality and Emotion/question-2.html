Approach

Displacement is shifting the blame onto another person. Projection is close, but often involves shifting one's thoughts onto someone else.

Displacement is often physical, such as taking out anger on a friend or pet. Projection involves thoughts and feelings, such as thinking someone hates you when it really is the other way around.
