Conditioning is not all equal. Animals learn things that they would naturally do faster. For humans, fear of rodents may have evolutionary origins since rodents often carry diseases. Our ancestors likely were afraid of rodents as well. Cars are a rather new invention and although they're responsible for more deaths than rodents (and many other common phobias), cars are commonplace and not readily feared.
